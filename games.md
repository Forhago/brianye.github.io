## Gods and Mortals

[![Slack for iOS Upload.jpg](https://s24.postimg.org/e8t9yfhqd/Slack_for_i_OS_Upload.jpg)](https://postimg.org/image/4o9nbjse9/)

Gods and Mortals is an chaotic, assymetric four-player fighting game. This is a final project we created in our game development class (Eecs 494). The four of us had to cooperate to create a fun, multiplayer game for the end-of-the-year showcase, where more than 200 people who attended.
